Jockey's Conference Center is a premier meeting and event venue located in the heart of Jakarta, Indonesia. Jockey's Conference Center is a popular choice for corporate conferences, trade shows, weddings and other social gatherings in Jakarta. It provides a convenient and well-equipped space with the latest in audio-visual technology and support. The venue has an on-site catering service offering a variety of local and international cuisine options.

Exhibition Halls

Jockey’s Conference Center offers two expansive exhibition halls, each accompanied by its own 1,000 sqm lobbies and access points. With a generous ceiling height of nine meters, these halls provide ample space for showcasing your exhibits. Equipped with essential amenities such as telephone and communication outlets, audio-visual points, and single and 3-phase power sources, we ensure seamless connectivity for your event. Additionally, floor box access allows for convenient access to electricity, phone lines, water, drainage, and compressed air upon request. Effortless loading and unloading are facilitated by direct truck access to both halls, enhancing logistical efficiency.

Our Story

With a rich history dating back to 1974, we boast over five decades of expertise in hosting events for renowned global influencers. Our exceptional facilities were initially crafted to cater to the prestigious 11th Non-Aligned Movement Summit and Asian-African Conference. Since then, we have continuously adapted to the latest advancements in event management and communication technology, ensuring a flawlessly executed experience from beginning to end.
