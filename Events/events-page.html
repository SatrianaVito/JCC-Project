Jockey's Conference Center is a premier meeting and event venue located in the heart of Jakarta, Indonesia. Jockey's Conference Center is a popular choice for corporate conferences, trade shows, weddings and other social gatherings in Jakarta. It provides a convenient and well-equipped space with the latest in audio-visual technology and support. The venue has an on-site catering service offering a variety of local and international cuisine options.
